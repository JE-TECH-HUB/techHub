// Our Mission
In today's fast-paced world, technology is at the heart of nearly every industry. From web development to data analysis, software engineering to digital marketing, the demand for tech-savvy professionals continues to grow.

At JE Tech Hub, we aim to bridge the gap between education and the ever-evolving tech landscape. Through our carefully designed courses and hands-on approach, we ensure that every learner gains real-world experience and knowledge they can immediately apply in their careers or entrepreneurial ventures.

We understand that learning technology can seem daunting, which is why we focus on creating an environment that nurtures curiosity and fosters growth. Whether you are a beginner looking to start your journey in the tech field or an experienced professional aiming to upskill, our mission is to provide you with the tools, guidance, and confidence you need to succeed.

Whether you're an entrepreneur looking to scale, a tech enthusiast eager to explore new frontiers, or someone seeking to upskill, we offer the knowledge, tools, and mentorship to help you thrive in the ever-evolving digital landscape.

// Our Journey
Founded in 2018 as JE Gadgets, we recognized the need for technical skills training in our community. Initially focused on smartphone repair and diagnostics, we trained 28 individuals, many of whom launched their own repair businesses. Over the years, we trained an additional 17 individuals in gadget repair.

In 2022/2023, we partnered with the National Information Technology Development Agency (NITDA) to deliver training programs in Kogi and Niger states, impacting 1,000-1,400 participants across a variety of digital and technical skills.

In 2023, we rebranded to JE Techhub to better reflect our expanded offerings, moving beyond repair training to include advanced IT and software development courses. Operating under the umbrella of JE Gadgets, we offer hands-on training with real-world equipment. Our sister brand, JE Logistics, supports us by providing logistics and delivery services, including computer equipment for our online training.

With 30 graduates and ongoing programs, JE Techhub continues to evolve, empowering individuals with essential digital skills. Our future vision includes expanding our online platform, forming strategic partnerships, and creating an innovation lab to foster local tech entrepreneurship.
